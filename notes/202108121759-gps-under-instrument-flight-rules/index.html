GPS under Instrument Flight Rules

GPSes certified under TSO-C129 and C196 are non-WAAS and TSO-C145 and C146 are WAAS augmented GPSes. WAAS stands for Wide Area Augmentation System, a separate network of satellites that transmit corrections to the GPS position from fixed ground stations.
